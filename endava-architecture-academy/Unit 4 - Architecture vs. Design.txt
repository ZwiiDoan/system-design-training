2. What is the difference between communication and collaboration?
    - communication is one of the collaboration activities which help different parties to exchange information. Other collaboration
    activities include but not limited to providing feedback on, updating or creating artefacts, dividing, assigning and directly
    working on tasks of the same job/common goal.
